# Product Vision

Bracco Process Hub is conceived as an internal operating system for cross-functional approvals. The platform is not just a form queue. It is a shared decision layer for teams working across countries, languages, and governance requirements.

## Core Problem

Commercial, procurement, finance, legal, and executive stakeholders often work on the same initiative with different timing constraints and document standards. The friction usually comes from fragmented visibility, duplicated packets, and inconsistent escalation rules.

## Product Direction

Bracco Process Hub should:

- make approval ownership visible from first request to executive sign-off
- standardize document templates without flattening country-specific needs
- provide AI-assisted sentiment and risk summaries for leadership
- support multi-country and multi-language operating models
- give management a clear view of bottlenecks before cycle time slips

## UX Principles

- Executive dashboards should feel calm, precise, and operational.
- Teams should understand what is blocked, who owns it, and what comes next.
- Automation should remove routine formatting and packet assembly before it automates judgment.
- Country teams should see their local reality without losing the regional view.

## Initial Outcome

This repository provides a credible starting point for:

- stakeholder demos
- architecture review
- design exploration
- Supabase data model discussions
- progressive implementation of real workflow logic
